Getting Started

Invite the Bot

Click the "Add to Discord" button on the homepage to add noobtube to your Discord server. Select the server you want and authorize the bot.

Join a Voice Channel

Connect to any voice channel in your Discord server. The bot will join you when you play a song.

Play Music

Use the command /play [youtube url] to start playing audio from YouTube in your voice channel.

all commands use discord's slash command system. type / in any channel to see available commands.

/play <url>

Play audio from a YouTube URL. The bot will join your voice channel and start playing.

Example: /play https://youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
/pause

Pause the currently playing track. Use /resume to continue playback.

/resume

Resume playback after pausing.

/skip

Skip the current track and play the next song in the queue.

/queue

Display the current music queue, showing what's playing and what's up next.

/nowplaying

Show information about the currently playing track.

/stop

Stop playback completely and clear the entire queue.

/leave

Make the bot leave the voice channel and disconnect.
